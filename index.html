Planet Enviro Services

Shaping a Cleaner, Greener, and Sustainable Future

A Pune-based environmental consultancy, Planet Enviro Services works with NABET-accredited EIA consultants to deliver sustainable and innovative environmental solutions. From Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) and Pollution Control to Water & Wastewater Treatment and Waste Management, we provide end-to-end solutions that ensure regulatory compliance and promote eco-friendly growth through advanced engineering and project management.

REGULATORY COMPLIANCE EXPERTS

Environmental Impact Assessment

An Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) is a systematic process to evaluate the potential positive and negative environmental, social, and economic effects of a proposed project or development before it is approved.

ENSURING COMPLIANCE FOR A GREENER FUTURE

Environmental Clearance and Compliance

An Environmental Clearance and Compliance note provides an overview of the regulatory process required for projects that may impact the environment, including the necessary approvals and ongoing monitoring obligations.

SUSTAINABLE SOLUTIONS FOR A BETTER FUTURE

Sustainability Services

Sustainability Services are a range of consulting and technical offerings that help organizations improve their environmental, social, and governance (ESG) performance, meet sustainability goals, and comply with regulations.

SUSTAINABLE WATER SOLUTIONS

Water & Wastewater Treatment

Water treatment involves purifying raw water for human use, while wastewater treatment removes contaminants from used water to make it safe for environmental release or reuse

MONITORING TODAY FOR A SAFER TOMORROW

Environmental Monitoring

Environmental Monitoring is the systematic process of observing, measuring, and analyzing the components of the natural environment including air, water, soil, and biodiversity—to understand current conditions, detect changes over time, identify potential problems caused by human activities, and predict future environmental trends
